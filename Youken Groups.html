OUR HISTORY

Youken Groups® Music Production Company was founded in 2019 by Youdom Ulrich Yann ( Youken ), a 17-year-old boy who had a dream to make artists proud of their talents and of how they could change the world's way to see, understand and listen to music.

"Music has been a great passion for me, the first person who inspired me is my elder sister, as she did the choir when she sings I could fly away like an angel, then I join the choir too. And that is when my big love started to because more powerful. Then from there by 2014 I had to move and I left the choir but in 2018, when I met Pierrad David my best friend he led me to French Rap music and we started to write songs together. From then when I get to Canada I started to produce beats and my Instagram followers encouraged me to continue and to do better and to be the best producer. From this, I had the idea to create a label called Youken."
